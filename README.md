## 🌟 What Is This?

Toontown Spirits is a nonprofit, fan-built MMO built on the foundation of Disney's Toontown Online. It keeps everything you loved — gag tracks, Cogs, cartoon chaos — and adds:

- A **physical inventory system** (Baggies, because hammerspace is terrifying)
- A **keychain charm system** for stat tuning and world progression
- **WRPG-style turn-based combat** with real RPG stats and D&D-style levelling
- **Cafes** as social hubs, crafting spots, and checkpoints
- **Expanded Toon customisation** including new species (yes, Capybara is in)
- **Guild-based group battles** and consensual PvP Training Arenas
- **Ace Ensembles** — matching outfits with a little sparkle effect
- A planned **3DS port**

## 🎮 Core Systems At A Glance

### Baggies
Your gag inventory. Physical. On your back. Visible on your model at all times. Upgradeable. Covered in keychains. Starts as a Simple Rucksack.

### Keychains
Charms that dangle off your Baggie. Earned from Cog Towers and events. Five types:

| Type | Does What |
|------|-----------|
| **Stats** | HP, ATK, DEF, Speed, Laff tweaks |
| **Drops** | Jellybean and item drop rates |
| **Discounts** | % off gags and clothes |
| **Award** | Unlocks new Cog Towers and districts |
| **Decor** | Absolutely nothing. Just vibes. |

Max 10 slots per Baggie. Max 2 of each type. All equipped keychains are physically visible on your model (LOD-reduced on 3DS).

### Combat
Turn-based, WRPG-flavoured. Classic gag tracks. RPG stats. D&D-style levelling. Gags used in battle are consumed. Wipe? Back to the last Cafe you visited.

Hold `Alt` to tiptoe. Every species has their own tiptoe animation. This is non-negotiable.

### Cafes
The heart of each district. Order snacks to restore HP. Pick up side quests. Meet people. Head upstairs to craft gag upgrades from recipes you've found in the world.

### Species
**Classic:** Dog, Cat, Mouse, Rabbit, Duck, Monkey, Bear, Pig, Horse, Deer

**New:** Fox, Wolf, Lynx, Capybara

**Special:** Axolotl *(Kofi supporters only)* · Human *(devs only)*
